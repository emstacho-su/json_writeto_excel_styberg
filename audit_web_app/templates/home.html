Null Hypothesis Testing for ISO 9001 Compliance

Conceptual Overview

We apply this to each ISO 9001 clause that should be covered by the work instruction:

Null Hypothesis (H₀): The document fully satisfies Clause X.Y requirements.
Alternative (H₁): A significant compliance gap exists.

Statistical tests are derived from either:

  • Direct absence/presence of requirements
  • Content quality, weighted by:
    • Direct quotes (strongest)
    • Paraphrased alignment
    • Cross-document linkage

p-values indicate whether the null hypothesis can be rejected (gap exists) or not.
